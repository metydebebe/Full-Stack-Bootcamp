Tips for Taking Care or your Pets

Here are some usefull tips to taking better care of your pets

  1. Take them on walks
  2. Feed them in regular intervals
  3. Make sure they are fully vaccinated

Creating a good home for you pet

Your pets need to have a place where they can eat and a place for them to sleep.


Daily schedule for your pet

Morning Afternoon Evening
Walk Rest Eat
Eat Eat Drink
Drink Rest Sleep

Make sure to follow this schedule exactly and everyday
